The Audit Trail and Recovery module was developed to improve system reliability and data traceability. As the Attendance System is used for daily operations, accidental data modification or deletion could affect attendance records and administrative processes. The module records historical information for supported system operations and maintains logs of important data changes. Administrators can review previous actions, monitor record history, and identify changes made within the system. To further improve data protection, recovery functionality was introduced through recycle bin and restoration features. Deleted records can be reviewed and restored when necessary, reducing the risk of permanent data loss. This functionality provides additional control over administrative operations and supports system maintenance activities.
