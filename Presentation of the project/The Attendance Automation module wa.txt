The Attendance Automation module was developed to reduce manual attendance checking and improve the accuracy of attendance records. The system runs automatically every morning to finalize the previous day’s attendance and prepare attendance records for the current day. The automation first reviews yesterday’s records. Employees who did not check in or check out are marked as absent, while employees who checked in but did not check out are recorded as missing check-out cases. After that, the system checks today’s attendance-required employees. It evaluates shift assignments, day-off schedules, public holidays, leave records, and half-day leave records to determine whether each employee is required to work. If an employee has approved leave, day off, or holiday status, the system records the correct attendance condition automatically. For employees who are required to work, the system monitors whether attendance has been recorded. If no valid check-in is found after the required time, the system creates a missing check-in placeholder. This helps Human Resource staff review attendance issues more easily. Overall, this module reduces repetitive HR work, improves attendance consistency, and ensures attendance statuses are processed according to company rules.
