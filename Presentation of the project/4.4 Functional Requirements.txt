4.4 Functional Requirements

The functional requirements define the services and operations that the Attendance System must provide to support attendance management and Human Resource operations.
1. The system shall allow administrators to create, update, and manage user accounts and access permissions.
2. The system shall support role-based access control for administrators, managers, and other authorized users.
3. The system shall manage organizational information including companies, branches, departments, teams, positions, and employee records.
4. The system shall allow employees to perform attendance check-in and check-out through the Telegram Mini App.
5. The system shall validate attendance records using QR code and authorized network information.
6. The system shall allow administrators to configure attendance QR codes and network validation settings.
7. The system shall manage employee shift schedules and shift templates.
8. The system shall allow managers to create, review, and publish team shift plans.
9. The system shall support shift assignment and calendar-based schedule visualization.
10. The system shall manage leave types, leave balances, leave records, and leave summaries.
11. The system shall support half-day leave processing and leave balance calculation.
12. The system shall generate attendance reports including daily reports, monthly reports, and attendance deduction reports.
13. The system shall provide filtering and export functions for attendance reports.
14. The system shall automatically process attendance status based on attendance records, leave records, holidays, and shift assignments.
15. The system shall automatically detect employee absences and missing check-out records through attendance automation.
16. The system shall maintain audit history and provide data recovery functionality for supported records.
17. The system shall allow employees to view their attendance records through the Telegram Mini App.

### 4.5 Non-Functional Requirements

The non-functional requirements describe the quality attributes and operational characteristics required by the Attendance System.

1. The system shall provide secure authentication and authorization mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.

2. The system shall enforce role-based access control to ensure users can only access permitted functions and data.

3. The system shall maintain data consistency across attendance, shift, leave, and reporting modules.

4. The system shall provide reliable attendance processing and status calculation according to defined business rules.

5. The system shall support attendance validation through QR code and network verification to improve attendance security.

6. The system shall provide audit and recovery mechanisms to improve data traceability and reduce the risk of data loss.

7. The system shall support large attendance datasets through optimized search, filtering, and reporting operations.

8. The system shall provide report export functionality in user-friendly formats such as CSV and Excel.

9. The system shall provide a responsive and user-friendly interface for both administrative users and employees.

10. The system shall support integration with Telegram Bot and Telegram Mini App platforms.

11. The system shall support automated attendance processing to reduce manual Human Resource operations.

12. The system shall be maintainable and extensible to support future business requirements and system enhancements.

13. The system shall provide high availability during normal organizational working hours.

14. The system shall support deployment and operation within a containerized environment using Docker technologies.
