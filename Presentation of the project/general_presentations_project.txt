2.1 General Informaiton of Project
For this internship, I worked on building and maintaining the internal company Attendance System. This system is used to track attendance for all staff, not only at the head office but also across multiple branches in Cambodia.

The system records attendance by using QR code scanning through the Telegram Mini App. It also handles employees who work under both flexible shifts and fixed shifts, which makes the project more complex. The system supports HR operations related to attendance management and is also useful for payroll, because employee attendance is directly related to salary calculation.

This project was not newly developed by me. It has been developed for over two years by three previous developers, and I am the fourth person to handle and continue improving this project. The system includes the Back Office admin panel, backend API, Telegram Mini App, and automation processes.

The project focuses on improving the user experience and making attendance management easier for employees and HR staff. Since Telegram is widely used in Cambodian society, the system uses an existing familiar platform to create a practical digital solution for attendance management.
