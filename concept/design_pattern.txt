• Design Pattern Report for attendance-api

  This project mainly follows Django’s standard web architecture, but several software design patterns can still be
  identified from the codebase. The strongest patterns are Layered Architecture, Chain of Responsibility, and a Facade-
  like service layer. Other patterns such as Decorator and Repository appear only partially and are not dominant across
  the system.

  Conclusion

  The most appropriate patterns to highlight for this project are Layered Architecture, Chain of Responsibility, and
  Facade. These patterns are clearly visible and consistently used in the codebase. Decorator and Repository can also be
  mentioned, but only as partial or limited implementations rather than core architectural choices.

  Suggested final sentence for your report

  The attendance-api project primarily applies Layered Architecture, Chain of Responsibility, and Facade-style service
  abstraction. These patterns help separate concerns, simplify complex business workflows, and structure request
  handling in a maintainable way, while Decorator and Repository appear only in limited form.
